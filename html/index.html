Shadow Jago's INSTINCT!

Shadow Jago can use his instinct for two purposes. The first is Dark Tether.
Dark Tether to his opponent by hitting them with a fireball or dashing through
their body. Once tethered to his opponent, pressing HP+HK will instantly generate
a stock of shadow meter for both Shadow Jago and his opponent. Also while
tethered, Shadow Jago's surges and shadow moves will require half the amount
as normal, while draining half of the cost of the move from the opponent's
shadow meter. The second use of Dark Tether is at any point during his
instinct, Shadow Jago can "cash out" the remainder of his instinct to perform
a Raging Demon. The damage of this is reflective of the amount of time left
in instinct.
